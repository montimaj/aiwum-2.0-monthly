Agriculture and Aquaculture Flowmeter data, Arkansas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Mississippi, Tennessee, Missouri, Alabama

This is a general repository for agriculture and aquaculture flowmeter data for Alabama, Arkansas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Missouri, and Tennessee

Description of Child Items:

Arkansas Static Metered Data: This data comes from metered sites in the U.S. State of Arkansas.  Data includes location, start reading date, end reading date, crop type, volume pumped, acreage, and application depth

Mississippi Voluntary Meter Program Annual Flowmeter Data: This data comes from metered sites in the U.S. State of Mississippi. Data includes location, start reading date, end reading date, crop type, volume pumped, acreage, and application depth

Real-time Flowmeter Data: This data comes from real-time flowmeter sites in the U.S. States of Alabama, Arkansas, Louisiana, Mississippi, Missouri, and Tennessee which is housed in the United States Geological Survey National Water Information System. 
Data includes location, daily withdrawal values, crop type, monthly totals, yearly totals, growing season (April 1 to September 30) totals, acreage, and application depth
